This module teaches a steward how to isolate a problem population instead of browsing the instance manually.

## Learning objective

Form a hypothesis, narrow the result set, and gather evidence before making a stewardship decision.

## What to learn

- simple vs complex filters;
- AND/OR grouping;
- the difference between broad search and precise filtering;
- why saved searches are a steward productivity tool.

![Basic filters]({{ "/assets/images/key-terms-and-features/filters-5.png" | relative_url }})

![Advanced filters]({{ "/assets/images/key-terms-and-features/filters-6.png" | relative_url }})

## Exercises

1. Create one simple filter to isolate a suspicious record set.
1. Create one grouped filter using both **AND** and **OR**.
1. Save the filter configuration as a reusable search.
1. Explain why the filter is narrow enough to support stewardship work.

## Steward habit to reinforce

Do not jump from “I think there is a problem” to “I will fix records.” First prove the population and understand the pattern.
